Publications & Preprints

Explaining Scenic Structure via Geometric Regularity
Preprint, 2026
Sayan Patra, Junjun Yin

Abstract: This work formalizes spatial coherence through measurable geometric invariants derived from structural regularity. The framework constructs feature mappings that enable interpretable prediction of scenic perception and provides conditioning mechanisms for generative vision models. The approach establishes a bridge between geometric structure, human perceptual response, and probabilistic generative systems.

Neural Population Dynamics for Adaptive Control
Working Paper, 2026
Sayan Patra

Abstract: This work develops a hierarchical control framework derived from neural population dynamics. Temporal structure observed in biological systems is translated into computational models that enable adaptive control in embodied agents operating under uncertainty. The framework integrates representation learning, dynamical systems modeling, and probabilistic inference to achieve robust sensorimotor adaptation in non-stationary environments.
