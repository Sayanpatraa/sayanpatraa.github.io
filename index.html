I develop principled learning systems under uncertainty. Current work seeks a unified framework for neuromorphic architectures operating on non-Euclidean domains with explicit modeling of aleatory and epistemic uncertainty.

Methodologically, this involves geometric deep learning, structured generative models, and non-smooth game-theoretic optimization in high-dimensional systems.

The objective is robust inference, interpretable generative structure, and stable multi-agent equilibria in adaptive environments.
